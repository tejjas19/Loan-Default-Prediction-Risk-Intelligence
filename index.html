Outlier Treatment
Applied IQR method (Q1 − 1.5×IQR, Q3 + 1.5×IQR) for income and loan amount features. Used Z-score (|z| > 3) for credit score and DTI ratio. Rows with extreme outliers capped or removed — 3.2% of the dataset.
IQR Capping: 1.9% Z-score Removal: 1.3%
Correlation Pruning
Removed 9 features with pairwise correlation > 0.85 using a greedy algorithm retaining the feature with higher target correlation. Reduced from 31 → 22 features.
